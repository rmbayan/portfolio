My Career

My dream career is to become a doctor, who interacts with their patients, diagnosing medical problems, and successfully treating the injury/disease. I choose this career because I want to help take good care of people's health and overall well-being. Some of my family members are in the medical field. I could see myself in this career because I know that if I study hard, I can make it. I will use my family members as my inspiration.
