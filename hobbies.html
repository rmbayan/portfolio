My 3 favorite hobbies to do are: drawing, game developing, and animating!


Drawing

I love to draw! Drawing is very fun for me! For me, I prefer to draw digitally than on paper. The art program I currently use for digital art is called "Medibang Paint Pro". I used to use "Paint S" as my art program, but I switched over to "Medibang Paint Pro" to try out its features, which "Paint S" does not include. Even though I still "Medibang Paint Pro" more, I use "Paint S" to make very quick sketches.

Here, you will see three of my digital artworks I created!


Game Developing

I love to develop many games! I love to code games that many people can enjoy! The program I use to develop games is "Roblox Studio". This program uses "LUA" for code! I have so much unfinished projects! ;D

Currently, my main focus is to finish a "Build to Survive" game with my online friends! (that involves memes hehe XD)


Animating

I love to animate! Even though it may take a while to get the animations look awesome, it is worth it at the end! The program I use to develop animations is "Roblox Studio".
